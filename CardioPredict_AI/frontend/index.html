Architecture

Full-Stack Data Flow

From form submission to prediction — everything runs on your infrastructure.

01

Form Input

User fills in 14 clinical, lifestyle, and genetic fields in the browser.

02

JSON POST

Frontend sends a fetch() to your Flask backend at /predict.

03

Feature Engineering

Backend builds derived features (BP category, lifestyle index) and applies your scaler.

04

Model Inference

Your trained .h5 or .pkl model produces a probability score.

05

SHAP + Output

SHAP values, recommendations, and clinical insight are assembled and returned as JSON.
